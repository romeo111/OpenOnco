Evidence summaryMSH6 somatic loss-of-function in crc produces dMMR/MSI-H phenotype. Pan-tumor MSI-H ICI eligibility (pembrolizumab per KEYNOTE-158, dostarlimab per GARNET) supersedes tumor-specific lines via tissue-agnostic FDA approval. 1L mCRC: pembrolizumab monotherapy (KEYNOTE-177) — mPFS 16.5 vs 8.2 mo vs chemo. Adjuvant ICI in stage III dMMR CRC under investigation; deferral of adjuvant 5-FU appropriate in stage II dMMR (chemoinsensitive). Neoadjuvant dostarlimab in dMMR rectal cancer → 100% cCR (Cercek 2022).

Notes

Somatic MSH6 loss → cascade testing optional (reflex germline confirmation strongly recommended; ~30% of dMMR tumors have germline cause). Pan-tumor MSI-H ICI eligibility supersedes tumor-specific lines.
