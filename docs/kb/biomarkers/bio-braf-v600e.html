Notes

Defining feature of HCL (~100%). Targetable with BRAF inhibitors (vemurafenib, dabrafenib) — useful in relapsed/refractory HCL when cladribine fails. Also relevant in melanoma, some thyroid, colorectal cancers (different therapeutic context).
