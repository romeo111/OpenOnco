Notes

FGFR2 fusion present in ~10–20% of intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma — the canonical actionable alteration in this disease. Pemigatinib (FIGHT-202) and futibatinib (FOENIX-CCA2) FDA-approved for advanced cholangiocarcinoma harboring FGFR2 fusion or rearrangement after ≥1 prior systemic therapy. Resistance: gatekeeper (V564F) and molecular-brake mutations — futibatinib (covalent) retains activity against some pemigatinib-resistance variants. FGFR2 amplification also a niche signal in gastric and breast.
