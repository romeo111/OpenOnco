Notes

Cross-disease relevance: - **CLL/SLL** (~10-15%): adverse prognostic marker — shorter OS, higher transformation rate to Richter (DLBCL). Often co-occurs with trisomy 12 + IGHV-unmutated. Modest impact on 1L choice in BTKi era but flag for early Richter watch. - **MCL** (~5-10%): adverse subset; may favor BTKi over chemoimmuno. - **T-ALL** (~50%): defining, drives γ-secretase inhibitor research. Currently informational in CLL/MCL; not direct algorithm driver. Future: 2L+ stratification + earlier Richter surveillance.
