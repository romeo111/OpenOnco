DefinitionGermline BRCA1 or BRCA2 pathogenic variant in HER2-negative breast cancer (HR+ or TNBC). Olaparib (OlympiAD — mPFS 7.0 vs 4.2 mo vs chemo) and talazoparib (EMBRACA — mPFS 8.6 vs 5.6 mo) are FDA-approved for metastatic gBRCA HER2-negative breast. Adjuvant olaparib (OlympiA) for high-risk HER2-negative gBRCA early breast.

Notes

Germline-only — somatic BRCA1/2 in breast lacks the same prospective RCT evidence (use HRD assay where pathway-altered status sought). Genetic counseling mandatory (cascade testing of first-degree relatives; risk-reducing salpingo-oophorectomy). PALB2 has analogous HRR-deficient biology — emerging data support PARPi but off-label. Toxicity: cytopenias (~grade 3 anemia 16-25%), MDS / AML <1.5% long-term. Test before starting any PARPi candidate (chemotherapy de-escalation argument: avoid platinum-based sensitizing regimens if PARPi readily available).
