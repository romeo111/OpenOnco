### IND-PV-2L-RUXOLITINIB
- **Regimen:** REG-RUX-PV  |  **Line:** 2  |  **Track:** aggressive

- **Review status:** pending_clinical_signoff
- **KB sources cited:** SRC-NCCN-MPN-2025, SRC-RESPONSE-VANNUCCHI-2015, SRC-ESMO-MPN-2015

**Current KB rationale:**

  Ruxolitinib is standard 2L for PV with hydroxyurea resistance or intolerance per ELN
  criteria. Pivotal RESPONSE phase-3 (Vannucchi et al., NEJM 2015): composite primary
  response (Hct control without phlebotomy + ≥35% spleen volume reduction at week 32) 21% vs
  1% with best available therapy (p<0.001). Particularly effective for symptom burden
  (pruritus, night sweats, fatigue) and splenomegaly which HU does not address. CRITICAL:
  NEVER abrupt-stop — taper over ≥1-2 weeks (cytokine rebound + spleen flare can be life-
  threatening). Mandatory pre-treatment HBV / HCV / HIV / TB screening. Add aspirin 81-100
  mg PO daily for thrombosis prophylaxis. Major Ukraine access barrier: registered but NOT
  НСЗУ-reimbursed for PV.

### IND-PMF-MOMELOTINIB-ANEMIA

**Current KB rationale:**

  often worsens cytopenia. Distinct ACVR1 inhibition reduces hepcidin → restores iron

  response 25% vs 9% (p=0.0095); spleen volume response 22% vs 3%; transfusion independence
