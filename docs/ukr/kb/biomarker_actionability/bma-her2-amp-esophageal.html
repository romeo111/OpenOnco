Підсумок доказівHER2-positive esophageal/GEJ adenocarcinoma scored by gastric criteria: trastuzumab + chemotherapy is preferred 1L (cross-referenced from ToGA / KEYNOTE-811) per SRC-NCCN-ESOPHAGEAL-2025, SRC-ESMO-ESOPHAGEAL-2024. Pembrolizumab + trastuzumab + fluoropyrimidine/platinum is FDA-approved for HER2+ PD-L1 CPS≥1 metastatic GEJ adenocarcinoma (KEYNOTE-811). Trastuzumab deruxtecan is FDA-approved 2L+ for HER2-positive advanced gastric/GEJ adenocarcinoma (DESTINY-Gastric01 includes Siewert types). Squamous-cell esophageal carcinoma is HER2-negative as a rule and is not in scope for HER2-directed therapy.

Нотатки

ESCAT IA / OncoKB Level 1. HER2 testing for adenocarcinoma histology only (not squamous). Heterogeneity high — ≥4 biopsies recommended. Source-gap: SRC-TOGA-BANG-2010, SRC-KEYNOTE-811, SRC-DESTINY-GASTRIC01 not ingested.
