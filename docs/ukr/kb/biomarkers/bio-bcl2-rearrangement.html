Нотатки

Defining feature of FL (~85% — t(14;18) IGH/BCL2). Component of double-hit (HGBL-DH) when co-occurring with MYC rearrangement — worse prognosis than either alone, drives DA-EPOCH-R over R-CHOP. Rare standalone in DLBCL (~20-30%). Triple-hit (with BCL6 also) rarest + worst.
