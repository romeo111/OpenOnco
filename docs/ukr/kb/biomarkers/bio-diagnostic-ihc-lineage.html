Нотатки

Composite vocabulary entry for diagnostic lineage and differentiation IHC markers. These markers support histogenesis, site-of-origin, lymphoma immunophenotype, neuroendocrine differentiation, melanoma lineage, mesothelial lineage, sarcoma lineage, and germ-cell differentiation. They should be accepted by free-text case parsing as biopsy context, but should not be interpreted as variant-level actionability or used alone to select targeted systemic therapy.
