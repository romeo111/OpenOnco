Нотатки

Defining feature of HGBL-DH/THL. MYC + BCL2 most common. MYC + BCL6 less common. Triple-hit (all three) rarest, worst prognosis. MUST test on every newly-diagnosed DLBCL — missed identification leads to under-treatment with R-CHOP.
