Нотатки

~25% of newly diagnosed AML; high allelic ratio (≥0.5) historically associated with poorer outcomes (risk-stratifying in ELN-2017; ELN-2022 reclassified all FLT3-ITD as intermediate when NPM1-mutant irrespective of AR). Midostaurin + 7+3 (RATIFY) and quizartinib + 7+3 (QuANTUM-First) FDA-approved 1L. Gilteritinib (ADMIRAL) approved for relapsed/refractory FLT3-mutated AML. FLT3-ITD is also a maintenance target post-allo-HSCT (sorafenib SORMAIN; gilteritinib BMT-CTN-1506).
