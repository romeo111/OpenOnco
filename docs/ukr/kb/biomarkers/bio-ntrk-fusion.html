Нотатки

Tumor-agnostic FDA approval for larotrectinib (NAVIGATE / SCOUT) and entrectinib (STARTRK-1/2 + ALKA) for NTRK-fusion-positive solid tumors after progression or where no satisfactory alternative exists. Fusion frequency: ~0.3% of common adult tumors but >90% in rare entities — secretory carcinoma of salivary gland (MASC), infantile fibrosarcoma, congenital mesoblastic nephroma, secretory breast carcinoma. Pan-TRK IHC is a reasonable inexpensive screen but RNA-NGS remains the gold standard. Resistance: solvent-front (G595R, G623R) and gatekeeper (F589L) mutations on progression — selitrectinib / repotrectinib in development.
