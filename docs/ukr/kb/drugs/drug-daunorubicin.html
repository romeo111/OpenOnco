Дані про препарат

КласAnthracycline (topoisomerase II inhibitor + DNA intercalator)
Механізм діїIntercalates into DNA, inhibits topoisomerase II, generates free radicals. Cumulative cardiotoxicity dose-dependent. Backbone of AML 7+3 induction.
Типове дозуванняAML 7+3 induction: 60 mg/m² IV days 1-3 (standard); 90 mg/m² days 1-3 (intensified, ECOG-ALL trial — younger fit patients <60). Vyxeos (liposomal): 44 mg/m² daunorubicin + 100 mg/m² cytarabine days 1, 3, 5 for tAML / AML-MRC.

Застереження

  • Cardiotoxicity (cumulative; risk rises sharply >550 mg/m²)
  • Severe myelosuppression
  • Vesicant — extravasation causes severe tissue necrosis

Нотатки

Backbone of 7+3 (cytarabine 100-200 mg/m² CIVI d1-7 + daunorubicin 60-90 mg/m² d1-3). Idarubicin 12 mg/m² is an interchangeable alternative (especially in some European protocols). Baseline LVEF + lifetime anthracycline dose tracking mandatory.
