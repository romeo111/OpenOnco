Дані про препарат

Класcorticosteroid — synthetic long-acting glucocorticoid
Механізм діїBinds the glucocorticoid receptor; in myeloma cells induces apoptosis through suppression of NF-κB signaling and direct effects on glucocorticoid-responsive plasma cell pathways. Synergistic with proteasome inhibitors and IMiDs.

Нотатки

In frail / elderly (>75 yrs) patients, weekly dose reduced to 20 mg to improve tolerability without compromising efficacy (FRAIL / IFM/HOVON data). PJP prophylaxis indicated when used at induction doses for ≥4 weeks combined with other immunosuppressives.
