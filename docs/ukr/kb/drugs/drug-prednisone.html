Нотатки

Equivalent to prednisolone (same active drug); used interchangeably. In R-CHOP, 5-day pulse minimizes steroid toxicity. Long-term high-dose use → PJP prophylaxis (cotrimoxazole). PPI co-prescription if GI risk factors. Glucose monitoring in diabetics.
