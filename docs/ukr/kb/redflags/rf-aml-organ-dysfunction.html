Нотатки

Triggers de-escalation from 7+3 + (midostaurin) to ven+aza (VIALE-A) for unfit patients. Transplant-eligibility is a parallel layer also captured by this flag (organ thresholds are the same fitness gate). Cardiac assessment + baseline echo mandatory before anthracycline. STUB — requires clinical co-lead signoff before publication.
