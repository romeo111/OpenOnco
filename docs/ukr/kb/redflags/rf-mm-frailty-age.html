Нотатки

IMWG frailty score (Palumbo 2015) is validated against survival on novel-agent regimens. Frail patients on full-dose VRd have ≈15-20% treatment-related mortality vs ≈3% on dose-modified schedules. Dose-modified VRd (V weekly, R 15-25 mg, dex 20 mg weekly) preserves efficacy with substantially better tolerability. This RF de-escalates toward IND-MM-1L-VRD with dose-modified schedule (when high-risk cytogenetics + frailty co-occur — both step 1 and this trigger fire, intensify takes precedence per direction order; engine surfaces both).
